                  Glossary · use these words the same way

                  Formatcreative direction

                  The editorial style/template the partner runs — e.g. meme grid post, headline carousel, green-screen reel, 2-pic comparison. The shape of the content.

                  Content Typethe thing itself

                  The asset being created — e.g. Reel, grid post, story, carousel, YouTube short. The technical container.

                  Brand Pack

                  A signed-off asset bundle Pubity creates on behalf of a partner so we can deploy campaign content without round-tripping for every approval. Four states: not started → drafted → signed → active rotation.

                  Reliability score

                  Subjective 1–10 rating of how responsive + dependable a partner has been on past campaigns. ≥8 = top tier, 5–7 = workable but slow, <5 = unreliable.

                  Wedge / Bundle / Vertical

                  Vertical = the category (Movies, Sport). Bundle = the packaged pages + creators + products we sell. Wedge = a vertical we are actively pushing as a priority sale (currently Movies + Positive Content).

                  Pages vs Creators vs Extended Network

                  Pages = the partner Instagram/social pages. Creators = named individual talent. Extended Network = inventory products (Clipping Network, YouTube Pre-Roll, OOH, Pubity Plus, Insiders) — things that aren't pages or creators.
